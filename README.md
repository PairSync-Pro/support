## What is PairSync?

PairSync is a mobile app that teaches you about **pairs trading** - a market-neutral strategy used by professional traders. Think of it like watching two similar stocks that usually move together. When one gets ahead, there might be an opportunity for them to "catch up" to each other.

**What You Can Do:**
- 📊 **Learn from Real Data** - Explore statistical relationships between real stocks and crypto assets
- 🌍 **Global Markets** - Study pairs from US, Australian, Indian, and European markets
- 📈 **Track Historical Patterns** - See how pairs have behaved over time through educational backtests
- 🎓 **Build Knowledge** - Access comprehensive educational content on statistical arbitrage
- 💼 **Develop Skills** - Learn concepts used by quantitative hedge funds

**What PairSync is NOT:**
- ❌ **Not a broker** - We don't execute trades or hold your money
- ❌ **Not investment advice** - All content is educational; you make your own decisions
- ❌ **Not a guarantee** - Historical patterns don't guarantee future performance
- ❌ **Not automated trading** - This is a learning tool, not a trading robot
