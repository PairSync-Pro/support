## Common Questions Before Contacting Support

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered here:

### Account & Login
**Q: I can't log in / forgot my password**  
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. Check your email (including spam folder) for the reset link. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes, contact support.

**Q: I didn't receive the verification email**  
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If still not there, contact support with your registered email.

### Trial & Subscription
**Q: How do I start my free trial?**  
A: The 30-day trial starts automatically when you create an account. No credit card required upfront.

**Q: How do I subscribe after my trial ends?**  
A: Open the app → Navigate to the Subscription tab → Tap "Subscribe" → Complete Apple In-App Purchase.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → PairSync → Cancel Subscription. [Detailed guide →](../features/subscription.md)

**Q: I was charged but my subscription isn't active**  
A: Try "Restore Purchases" in the app (Subscription tab → Restore button). If that doesn't work, email us with your Apple receipt.

### Technical Issues
**Q: The app won't load / crashes on launch**  
A: Try: (1) Force quit and reopen, (2) Restart your device, (3) Update to latest iOS version, (4) Reinstall the app. If issue persists, contact support.

**Q: Data isn't refreshing**  
A: Pull down on the feed screen to manually refresh. Check your internet connection. If data shows "stale" indicator, it may be due to market being closed or backend maintenance.

**Q: I can't add pairs to my watchlist**  
A: Ensure you're logged in and have an active subscription or trial. If issue persists, contact support.

### Billing
**Q: I was charged but don't want the subscription**  
A: Cancel via iOS Settings (see above). For refund requests, contact Apple Support directly (Apple handles all payments and refunds).

**Q: I want a refund**  
A: Refunds are subject to Apple's refund policy. Request via: reportaproblem.apple.com. We cannot process refunds directly.
