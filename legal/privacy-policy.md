## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create a PairSync account, we collect:

**Required Information:**
- **Email address** (for account creation and communication)
- **Password** (hashed and encrypted - we never store plain text passwords)
- **Account creation date**
- **Trial start date**

**Optional Information:**
- **Display name** (if you choose to set one)
- **Profile preferences** (theme, notification settings)

### 1.2 Device Information
For technical functionality and trial abuse prevention, we collect:

- **Device fingerprint** (unique identifier based on device characteristics)
- **Device model** (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)
- **Operating system version** (e.g., iOS 17.4)
- **App version** (e.g., PairSync 1.2.3)
- **Device language and region settings**
- **Screen resolution and device type**

**Purpose:** Device fingerprinting prevents users from exploiting the 30-day free trial multiple times through different accounts on the same device.

### 1.3 Usage Data
To improve PairSync and understand user behavior, we collect:

- **App usage analytics:**
  - Screens viewed and time spent
  - Features used (watchlists, trade journal, feed tabs)
  - Button clicks and navigation patterns
  - Search queries within the app

- **Performance data:**
  - App crashes and error logs
  - Load times and performance metrics
  - API request/response times

- **Interaction data:**
  - Pairs added to watchlists (symbol data only, not your trading intentions)
  - Asset class preferences (stocks vs crypto)
  - Market geography selections

### 1.4 Network and Technical Data
- **IP address** (for security, fraud prevention, and approximate geolocation)
- **Network connection type** (WiFi, cellular)
- **Time zone**
- **Login timestamps and session duration**

### 1.5 Subscription Data
Processed by Apple and shared with us via RevenueCat:

- **Subscription status** (trial, active, expired, canceled)
- **Purchase date and renewal date**
- **Subscription tier** (currently only one tier)
- **Transaction IDs** (Apple-generated, not payment card details)

**We DO NOT collect or store:**
- Credit card numbers
- Billing addresses
- Payment method details (Apple handles all payment processing)

### 1.6 Communications
If you contact us via email or support:
- **Email content and attachments**
- **Support ticket history**
- **Correspondence records**

### 1.7 Data We DO NOT Collect
**PairSync explicitly DOES NOT collect:**
- ❌ Brokerage account credentials (we don't integrate with brokerages)
- ❌ Trading activity or positions at your brokerage
- ❌ Financial account balances or net worth
- ❌ Social Security numbers or tax IDs
- ❌ Precise geolocation (only approximate from IP)
- ❌ Contacts, photos, or microphone access
- ❌ Biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID is handled locally by iOS, not by us)

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security practices:

**Encryption:**
- **In transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS (encrypted connections)
- **At rest:** Database encryption for stored data (Neon PostgreSQL security)
- **Passwords:** Hashed using bcrypt or similar (never stored in plain text)

**Access Controls:**
- **Principle of least privilege:** Employees access only data necessary for their role
- **Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication for admin access
- **Logging:** Audit logs of data access and modifications

**Application Security:**
- **Regular updates:** Patching vulnerabilities in dependencies
- **Code reviews:** Security-focused code audits
- **Penetration testing:** Periodic security assessments (plan to implement)

### 4.2 Data Retention
- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Usage data:** Retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes
- **Support emails:** Retained for 3 years for legal and compliance purposes
- **After deletion:** Data permanently deleted within 90 days (except as legally required)

### 4.3 Limitations
**No system is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for:
- Keeping your password confidential
- Using a secure device
- Reporting suspected unauthorized access

**You have the right to:**

### 5.5 Restriction of Processing
**You have the right to:**
- Restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances (e.g., while disputing accuracy)

**How to request:** Email mark@pair-sync.com with specific restrictions requested.

### 5.6 Object to Processing
**You have the right to:**
- Object to certain data processing activities (e.g., analytics)

**How to request:** Email mark@pair-sync.com. We will evaluate your request based on legal grounds.
